Introduction

Spearheaded by a multi-pronged vision, this website serves three primary purposes.
Firstly, as a digital repository for me to chronicle my life, liberated of the limitations of corporeal vessels.
Secondly, as a sandbox in which I can develop my programming capabilities and express my creative paradigms.
Lastly, a muse for me to build, slowly but surely, a story of my philosophical and spiritual journey.

About Me

My name is Bohan Lin, or 林博涵。I am a conglomerate of many identities; I am a chemist, artist, bodybuilder, runner, partner...
...Yet those are only labels, and an individual is never merely their sum total. Instead, I invite you to explore my website, and experience, rather than read about,
who I am.
